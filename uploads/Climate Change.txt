User: What are the main causes?

User: Where do those gases come from?

AI: Mostly from burning coal, oil, and gas for energy, deforestation, agriculture, and industrial processes.

User: What kind of impact does this have?

AI: Rising sea levels, more extreme weather events, heatwaves, droughts, biodiversity loss, and threats to food and water security.
